About us

Our History

Starbucks was founded in 1971 in Seattle, United States, initially focused on selling coffee beans. Over the years, the company expanded its business model, began serving ready-made beverages, and grew internationally, becoming one of the largest coffee shop chains in the world.

Our Purpose

Starbucks aims to offer more than just coffee, providing a welcoming experience for its customers. The brand strives for quality in its products, creating comfortable environments and acting responsibly, with attention to sustainability and social impact.
